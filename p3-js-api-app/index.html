🎬 Movie Guessing Game Instructions

Welcome to the Movie Guessing Game! Here's how to play:

  • Guess the movie based on hints given by the game.
  • There are 5 hints per movie. Hints 1–4 reveal parts of the movie overview.
  • Hint 5 gives the release year and genres of the movie.
  • You have up to 5 guesses per movie.
  • A guess is correct if at least 2 words from the movie title match your input (1 word for single-word titles).
  • The game has multiple levels. Each level has a number of movies to attempt and a required score to progress.
  • Your score and progress are tracked throughout the levels.
  • At the end of each level, you can advance to the next level or restart if you didn’t meet the required score.
